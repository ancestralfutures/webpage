A Multi-sensory experience

Join us to celebrate diasprian talents and hounour our history by displaying our love and passion for the ones who came before us through arts and music. The four main artists performing tackle themes of African identity whilst embedding their innovative take on rap & alternative music.

The event is set in London's new 24hr nighclub in east London with state of the art audio and visual systems. The night is curated around african agricultural seasons, audience is taken on an immersive journey through planting to harvestation. Visual, scent, temparatue and taste elements are used to further enhance the music experience as each artist and track brings a new feeling.
