A multi-sensory experience

Join us to celebrate African Diaspora talents through music while displaying our love and admiration for the ones who came before us. Immersing the audience in a live music experience that engages the 5 senses.

The event takes inspiration from the Afro-Futurism movement that was pioneered by the the late Sun-Ra during the mid-fifties. Afrofuturism is a cultural aesthetic, philosophy of science and history that explores the developing intersection of African Diaspora culture with technology.
